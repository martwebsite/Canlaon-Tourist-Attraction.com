Canlaon City Tourist Attractions

What is Canlaon City known for?

Canlaon City cradles the most visited and most coveted Mount Kanlaon, one of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines. It serves as a striking landmark in the province of Negros Oriental because of its majestic shape and size.
